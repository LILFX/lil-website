NATURE AT ITS BEST

Karura forest is an urban forest located in the capital city of Kenya, Nairobi. Its breathtaking scenary is just but a little of what the magical forest is all about; It houses over 100 species of birds and small animals i.e;snakes, beavers and rodents just to mention a few.
It also has beautiful water bodies such as rivers and waterfalls.

Tourists are expected to pay a fee pf KSH.200 to tour the amazing forest. It has about 50km of trails which can be covered by both bycicles or on foot Those who like cycling have a wide variety of biccles to choose from @KSH.500 an hour. For security purposes an electric fence has been built around the forest and also security personnel have been deployed in different pats of this big forest.''
